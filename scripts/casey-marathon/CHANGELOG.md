### What changed

- **Voice swap.** Out: Daniel's observational deadpan. In: Casey-performing-Elle (affiliative + self-enhancing humor per Martin's four-style framework).
- **Five Legally Blonde anchors, not one.** v1 used only "What, like it's hard?". v2 stacks Elle's admission-video opener, the multi-cut credentials structure, Brooke Windham's endorphins monologue, a bend-and-snap Easter egg, and the Elle button.
- **Hard CTA with dollar tiers.** $20 = one mile. $100 = Spotify queue callback. $6,000 = goal. Replaces "link in bio" (~1% conversion) with specific-tier structure (~3-5x better).
- **Native UGC hook aesthetic.** Jump-cut structure across multiple locations/outfits. Matches the actual Elle admission video AND what's viral on TikTok right now.
- **New draft format.** Draft A is now a UGC mockumentary. Draft B is a direct Brooke Windham parody. Draft C upgrades the v1 committee into a Greek chorus that speaks in Legally Blonde quotes.
- **Added: CAPTION-COPY.md** — post captions, share prompts, DM templates, dollar-tier messaging, cross-post calendar, hashtag strategy.
- **Expanded: CALIBRATION.md** — now includes shot lists, joke-swap alternates, wardrobe checklist, post schedule.
- **Expanded: STRATEGIC-FRAMING.md** — Martin humor classification, v1→v2 diagnosis, funnel logic.
