# Draft B: The Short Loop

**Length:** 30 to 45 seconds
**Extras:** None (Casey solo)
**Best for:** Stories, repost, short-form grid content, a second post after the hero
**Status:** Draft, awaiting Casey's review

## Format

Shorter, tighter, no "admission video" scaffolding. Direct-to-camera monologue. Works as a loop or standalone.

## The script

### Hook (0 to 3 seconds)

> I'm running the New York City Marathon on November first. Twenty-six point two miles.

### Body

> My anticipated finish time is four hours and twenty-two minutes of continuous suffering, if my training is accurate, which it is not.
>
> I need to raise six thousand dollars for Release Recovery. They pay for addiction treatment when a person can't afford it.

### Subversion (around 60% mark)

> Six years ago, that person was me. I'm running the marathon to cover the cost for somebody else.

### Button

> Twenty-six miles. What, like it's hard?

## Delivery notes

Same rules as Draft A, tighter execution. This version trades the Elle "admission video" scaffolding for pure compression. Every second counts. Deliver at roughly 2 words per second. No dead air.

## Why this version exists

Some viewers see the long-form Draft A and share it. Some will only engage with a 30-second clip. This is the same story, loopable, gridable, story-postable.

**Pair with Draft A, don't replace it.** Post A as the main reel/TikTok. Post B a week later as a reminder, or cut it into Stories.
