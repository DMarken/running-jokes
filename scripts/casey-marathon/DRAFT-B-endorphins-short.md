# Draft B (v2): The Endorphins Short

**Length:** 30 to 40 seconds
**Format:** Single-take monologue with ONE outfit change at the button
**Production level:** Very low (phone, tripod or a friend to hold it, 10 minutes)
**Best for:** Stories, repost, TikTok short-form companion to Draft A
**Why v2 is different from v1:** v1 was a stripped-down version of Draft A. This v2 is structurally distinct — it's a DIRECT Brooke Windham "endorphins" monologue parody, the second most-quoted LB scene. Pairs with Draft A as its own content, not as a shorter clone.

---

## The script

### Shot 1 — Casey in workout clothes, mid-stretch or on a stationary bike, upbeat Brooke-Windham energy

**Casey:** "I'm running the New York City Marathon on November first. Twenty-six point two miles. Four hours and twenty-two minutes of continuous suffering, mostly voluntary."

### Shot 2 — Same location, energy holds

**Casey:** "I am raising six thousand dollars for Release Recovery. They cover addiction treatment for people who cannot afford to get in."

### Shot 3 — Energy drops, direct eye contact, flat delivery

**Casey:** "Six years ago that was me."

*(One beat.)*

### Shot 4 — Energy snaps back, Brooke Windham full parody, maybe a quick cut to Casey doing leg lifts or jumping jacks

**Casey:** "Exercise gives you endorphins. Endorphins make you happy. Happy people—"

*(Jump cut, Casey direct to camera, holds up pink placard that says "DONATE")*

**Casey:** "—donate to marathon fundraisers."

### Shot 5 — Final button, flat Elle

**Casey:** "Twenty-six miles. What, like it's hard?"

### End card

```
fundraisers.nyrr.org/casey-shrader
$20 = one mile
$6,000 = the full goal
LINK IN BIO
```

---

## Delivery notes

- **Pace is faster than Draft A.** You have 30 seconds and a full emotional arc to run. No breathing room. Every line moves.
- **The Brooke Windham parody** works only if you commit. Bright. A little too much. Workout video energy. It's the contrast with the reveal that sells the whole piece.
- **The reveal drops THEN the energy comes back.** This is the move. Most fundraiser content stays serious after the reveal. You refuse. The joke lifts the ask out of guilt territory.

---

## Why this version matters

- **Loopable.** TikTok weights rewatch at 200%. This whole video is ~35 seconds with a clear loop point ("Twenty-six miles. What, like it's hard?" → opening "I'm running the New York City Marathon"). Viewers who rewatch boost completion.
- **Story-post native.** Fits in a Story without trimming. Casey can post it to Instagram Stories with a "Link" sticker, which adds a second CTA surface.
- **Pairs with Draft A.** Post Draft A as the main reel. Post this 3-5 days later as a reminder with updated fundraising-total text overlay. Keeps the momentum.
